Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Jalpai Ganesh Puja Committee / জালপাই গণেশ পূজা কমিটি uses this official website.

Information shown on this website

The website may show public committee information, puja updates, event details, gallery photos, location information, donation-support instructions, and member verification cards.

Member verification

Member verification uses a member name and 4-digit code, such as Chandra 2580. The result is intended to show only the member photo, member name, and MEMBER label.

Member photos

Member photos are loaded from the website folder using the format images/members/Name Code.jpg. If a member photo is not uploaded, the website may show a fallback message.

Personal information

This website does not ask visitors to submit sensitive personal information. No public phone number, WhatsApp number, UPI ID, or direct payment form is provided on this website.

Donation / support communication

Donation support is handled only through the official Facebook DM/message link. The website may prepare a donation message for the visitor to copy or send through Facebook DM. The website itself does not collect money.

Photos and gallery

Gallery photos are published for public puja memories and committee updates. For correction or removal requests, contact the committee through the official Facebook DM/message link only.

Official contact rule

Official communication is through Facebook DM only.
No phone number, WhatsApp number, or separate contact person is available.

Security note

Do not trust fake payment links, fake donation requests, fake notices, or unofficial contact numbers claiming to represent the committee. Confirm important matters through the official Facebook DM/message link only.
