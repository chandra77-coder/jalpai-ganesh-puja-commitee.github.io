Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Jalpai Ganesh Puja Committee uses this official website.

Information we show

This website may show public committee information, announcements, gallery photos, event information and member verification details.

Personal information

We do not ask visitors to submit sensitive personal information through this website. Member verification codes are used only to show matching member profile information stored in the website code.

Photos and gallery

Event photos are published for community memories and public updates. If any photo needs correction or removal, contact the committee directly.

Security note

Visitors should not trust fake payment links, fake donation requests or unofficial announcements outside the official committee communication channels.

Contact

For any privacy-related correction, contact Jalpai Ganesh Puja Committee through the official committee members.
