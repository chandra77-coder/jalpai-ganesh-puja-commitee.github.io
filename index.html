About Us

A trusted community puja committee

Our committee works to celebrate Shri Ganesh Puja with devotion, discipline, cultural unity and social responsibility.

Jalpai Ganesh Puja Committee represents a collective effort of local members, volunteers and supporters who come together every year to organize Ganesh Puja and community activities.

Through this website, visitors can check official announcements, puja information, cultural updates, event memories, and verified member information. This keeps communication clean, transparent and easy to access.
