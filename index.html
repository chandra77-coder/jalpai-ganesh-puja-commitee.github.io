Our Story

About The
Committee

The Jalpai Ganesh Puja Committee has been serving the Jalpai community with devotion and unity since 2020. The committee works through collective responsibility, not through a single post-based structure.

There is no public president, secretary, treasurer, phone number, WhatsApp number, or separate contact person. Official communication happens through Facebook DM only.
