About The Committee

The Jalpai Ganesh Puja Committee has served the Jalpai community with devotion and unity since 2020.

The committee works through collective responsibility. No public president, secretary, treasurer, phone number, WhatsApp number, or separate contact person is listed. Official communication happens through Facebook DM only.
